VPPaaS – Sprint 1
Entrega 1: Design do Fluxo de Informacao, Topicos Kafka e Microservices

Project: Virtual Power Plant as a Service (VPPaaS)
Architecture principle (lecturer clarification): Each microservice exclusively owns a single business entity and implements all logic over that entity (filters, analyses, min/max/avg). When data from different entities needs to be crossed, the Camunda business process orchestrates the calls to the various microservices. In Sprint 1, without Camunda, the calls are made directly via REST.

2. D1 – Entity Registration, Asset Link Activation and Telemetry Ingestion

D1 – Asset Registration, Asset Link Activation and Telemetry Ingestion

  • Prosumer MS and UtilityOperator MS (Pattern 1): REST + JDBC + RDS. No Kafka.
  • AssetLink MS (activation): Persists to RDS, creates Kafka topic via AdminClient.createTopics(), calls POST /CreateaTopicConsumer on Telemetry MS.
  • AssetLink MS (deactivation): Stops the DynamicTopicConsumer via POST /StopTopicConsumer, deletes the topic via AdminClient.deleteTopics(). Prevents "Zombie Assets" (project brief pg. 4).
  • Telemetry MS (only Kafka consumer): DynamicTopicConsumer extends Thread with KafkaConsumer.poll(100ms). Persists time-series data and exposes stats via REST.

3. D2 – Flexibility Emission

D2 – Flexibility Emission (entity: FlexibilityEvent)

  • Cross-entity (Camunda): Queries Telemetry MS and Prosumer MS and passes data to Flexibility MS via REST.
  • Internal logic: Arbitrage Logic (soc > 90% AND price HIGH → SELL), Balancing Logic (soc < 20% → UNAVAILABLE). Publishes to Flexibility Event topic.

4. D3 – Grid Balancing Recommendation

D3 – Grid Balancing Recommendation (entity: BalancingRecommendation)

  • Cross-entity (Camunda): Aggregates data from UtilityOperator MS, Telemetry MS, AssetLink MS and Prosumer MS (Figure 9 of the project brief).
  • Internal logic: Identifica zonas em deficit e surplus, publica em GridBalancingRecommendation.

5. D4 – Energy Analytics

D4 – Energy Analytics (entity: EnergyAnalytics)

  • Cross-entity (Camunda): Aggregates data from Telemetry MS, Prosumer MS and UtilityOperator MS.
  • 4 output topics (Figure 10): EnergyDischargedByZone, GeneratedEnergyByProsumer, ConsumedEnergyByProsumer, AverageSoC.

6. D5 – Flexibility Forecasting (AI / Ollama)

Internal extension of the Flexibility MS. No separate RDS, no separate topic. Ollama is called internally before publishing the FlexibilityEvent to the same topic as D2.

D5 – Flexibility Forecasting AI (extension of Flexibility MS, Figure 11)

  • No separate RDS: Uses the Flexibility MS RDS. Fields sentiment, successRate and aiPattern stored in the FlexibilityEvent entity.
  • No separate topic: Publishes to the same Flexibility Event topic as D2, with offerType: AI_DISCHARGE.
  • Ollama: Called internally via REST. No separate JDBC or Kafka (Figure 11).
